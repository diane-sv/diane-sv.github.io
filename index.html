Technology + Neuroscience = Work

Directed by Dr. Luke Macyszyn, the medical imaging lab produces machine learning apps for spine analysis. As their software developer, I create VR experiences for patient education, web apps, and training databases for ML, all within the past 2 years.

Under the director Dr. Nanthia Suthana, the research lab studies the neuroscience behind learning and memory. Dubbed their VR Magician, I have produced multiple experiences that include untethered walking and eye tracking within the past 2 years.
